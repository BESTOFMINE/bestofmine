Privacy Policy of BESTOFMINE (www.bestofmine.com)

This document talks about the kind of information we would receive from our visitors and the measures we take to safeguard the information.

The Contact Us form of BESTOFMINE requires visitors to fill in information like first name, last name, email, mobile, company, and describe your requirements. We understand the importance of this information, and this will be used only to contact the client to communicate about the service requested. We also respect the clients' communication methods and will not use or disclose the information without consent from them.

We would also be receiving information about the IP addresses of visitors to www.bestofmine.com. These are used only to analyze visitor behavior, popular pages, exit ratios, time spent on the pages, navigation, etc. These IP addresses are not linked to personal and identifiable information. Hence the IPs are available, but the personal information remains anonymous.

Any communication, including the client's identity, credit card information, strategies, and other confidential information, will be held in the utmost confidence and will be divulged only to those parties as requested by the client.



Cookies:

Our website uses cookies and text files placed on the server's hard disk of the visitors' computers. Cookies help in measuring visitor information, repeat visitors, analyzing statistics, and data trends. Cookies cannot be used to run programs and deliver viruses to the computer, and the information gathered using cookies is anonymous.

Visitors have the option of accepting or declining cookies. By default, all browsers accept cookies. But you could modify the browser settings to refuse or reject cookies. Please remember that this would affect web pages' customization and the interactive element you experience on this website and other websites.

If you find that we have not adhered to this privacy policy, please get in touch with us, and we would consider the matter with utmost seriousness.
